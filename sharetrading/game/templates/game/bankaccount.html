Bank Account

Welcome to the share trading game. Your job is to buy and sell the 3 companies below with the aim of making a profit. The share prices of the 3 companies will evolve over time - try to buy when the price is low, and sell when the price is high!

In this game, all companies have a slightly better chance of increasing in value than decreasing - so if you play enough times, you should end up in the green more often than not!

Careful - each trade (buy AND sell) costs $1. If you trade too much, you will eat away at your gains!


Start buying and selling!
